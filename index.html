Who We Are

A cart.
A craft.
A community.

Lune started with a simple belief: that a great cup of coffee doesn't need four walls and a storefront. It needs care, quality ingredients, and a genuine love for the craft.

We roll through Dallas bringing specialty espresso drinks to neighborhoods, events, markets, and mornings that deserve something better than average.

Every drink is made to order. Every visit is personal. That's the Lune way.

— Lune Coffee & Co.
